NIKE AIR FORCE 1

PRESENTING ‘FEEL FREE, LET’S TALK’, A SHOWCASE AIMED AT BRINGING YOU IN TO JOIN FORCES AND CELEBRATE THE PEOPLE AND PLACES THAT MAKE US

40TH ANNIVERSARY

CONCEPT

To celebrate the AF1’s 40th anniversary Nike came together to show the collective power of force. Presenting ‘Feel free, Let’s Talk’, a showcase aimed at bringing you in to join forces and celebrate the people and places that make us, Nike reignited Gallery on 4th in Melville for a weekend of talks about community with Wanda Lephoto, DBN GOGO and Anthony Bila.

CREATION

Our team was brought in to document the weekend- long exhibition which showcased; a variety of work from community heroes and pieces from emerging artists who, together with established creatives, express what it means to ‘Be True ‘ in South Africa.
